Sleeping Positions

The way you position yourself as you sleep can have great impact on your overall health. Below are the pros and cons of sleeping positions.

On Your Back

Pro

  • allows body to rest in neutral position
  • balances out your body weight
  • keeps your internal organs alinged
  • prevents neck and back pain

Cons

  • dangerous for those with sleep apnea
  • makes snoring more severe
  • causes periods of breathlessness

On Your Side

Pro

  • alleviates acid reflux and heartburn
  • keeps airways open
  • improves circulation

Cons

  • can put pressure on the stomach and lungs
  • arm numbness
  • puts a lot of stress on the shoulders

The Fetal Position

Pro

  • improves circulation
  • prevents uterus from presssing your liver
  • eases snoring

Cons

  • can restrict breathing in your diaphragm
  • can leave you feeling a bit sore in the morning

On Your Stomach

Pro

  • eases snoring

Cons

  • can lead to lower back problems
  • strains neck
  • puts pressure on muscles and joints
